# The Goblin Cave of Ashenvale

**Adventure Type**: Dungeon Crawl  
**Difficulty**: Beginner-friendly  
**Recommended Party Size**: 2–4 players  
**Recommended Level**: 1  

---

## Overview

A merchant's wagon was ambushed near the village of Millhaven. The merchant, **Aldric Thorn**, survived but lost his cargo — a chest containing 200 gold pieces and a family heirloom dagger. Tracks lead into the hills to the north, toward a cave system known locally as **the Ashenvale Burrows**, rumored to be inhabited by goblins.

The village elder has offered a reward of **50 gold pieces** to whoever retrieves the stolen goods.

---

## Available Player Classes

- **Fighter** — A strong melee warrior. Good for beginners. High HP and armor.
- **Wizard** — A powerful spellcaster. Low HP but can cast Sleep and Magic Missile.
- **Rogue** — A nimble trickster. Good at sneaking and dealing high damage in one strike.

## Available Races

- **Human** — Balanced stats, +1 to all ability scores.
- **Elf** — +2 Dexterity, +1 Intelligence, darkvision, advantage on Perception checks.
- **Dwarf** — +2 Constitution, +1 Wisdom, resistance to poison, darkvision.

**Aldric Thorn** (Quest Giver)
- Role: Merchant, lives in Millhaven
- Personality: Nervous, grateful, tends to over-explain
- Information he can provide: The ambush happened at dusk. He saw 4–5 goblins. They dragged the chest north toward the hills.

**Elder Mira** (Village Elder)
- Role: Quest authority, offers the reward
- Personality: Stern but fair, protective of Millhaven

### Monsters

**Goblin** (x6 total across all scenes)
- HP: 7
- Armor Class: 15
- Attack: Scimitar (+4 to hit, 1d6+2 slashing damage)
- Attack: Shortbow (+4 to hit, 1d6+2 piercing damage, range 80/320 ft)
- Abilities: Nimble Escape (can Disengage or Hide as a bonus action)
- XP: 50 per goblin

**Goblin Boss: Grix** (final enemy)
- HP: 21
- Armor Class: 17 (shield)
- Attack: Morningstar (+4 to hit, 2d6+2 piercing damage)
- Abilities: Redirect Attack (when hit, can redirect the attack to a nearby goblin), Multiattack (2 attacks per turn)
- XP: 200
- Carries: Aldric's heirloom dagger, key to the chest

## Scenes

---

### Scene 1: The Village of Millhaven

**Setting**: A small farming village at dusk. Aldric Thorn sits outside the inn, head bandaged, looking distressed. The village elder stands nearby.

**Objective**: Learn about the quest and agree to help.

**DM Notes**:
- Introduce the players to Aldric and Elder Mira.
- Aldric describes the ambush with a nervous, rambling energy.
- Elder Mira offers 50 gold as reward for the chest's return. The heirloom dagger is Aldric's personal request — no extra reward, but he will be very grateful.
- Players can ask questions. Aldric knows: direction (north, toward the hills), approximate number of goblins (4–5), time of attack (dusk, about 2 hours ago).
- Once players agree, proceed to Scene 2.

**Exits**: North road → Scene 2

---

### Scene 2: The Hillside Trail

**Setting**: A rocky trail winding up into the hills under a half-moon. Goblin tracks are visible in the mud. The cave entrance is visible ahead — a dark crack in the hillside with a crude wooden post bearing a skull.

**Objective**: Reach the cave. Optional: defeat or sneak past the 2 goblin sentries.

**Encounters**:
- 2 Goblins stand guard at the cave entrance.
- Players can attempt to sneak past (DC 13 Dexterity/Stealth check), distract the goblins, or fight them.
- If combat: goblins shout an alarm on their second turn, alerting goblins in Scene 3 (they will be ready and not surprised).

**Loot** (if sentries defeated):
- 3 gold pieces total
- 1 Healing Potion (tucked inside a small sack near the post)

**DM Notes**:
- Describe the eerie quiet of the trail, the smell of smoke from inside the cave.
- If players sneak successfully, they gain a surprise round in Scene 3.

**Exits**: Cave entrance → Scene 3

---

### Scene 3: The Goblin Warren

**Setting**: A low, torch-lit cave tunnel opening into a rough chamber. Crude bedrolls, gnawed bones, and stolen goods are scattered around. A locked wooden chest sits against the far wall. A heavy door to the north is bolted shut.

**Objective**: Defeat the goblins, retrieve the chest key from Grix (behind the bolted door), and open the chest.

**Encounters**:
- 3 Goblins are in the main chamber. If alarmed in Scene 2, they are behind cover (+2 AC) and act on the first round.
- The bolted door leads to Grix's chamber. Players must break it down (DC 14 Strength check) or pick the lock (DC 12 Dexterity/Thieves' Tools check) to reach him.
- 1 Goblin Boss Grix is in the back chamber, with 1 remaining Goblin bodyguard.

**Loot**:
- Chest (locked): 200 gold, 1 Healing Potion
- Grix carries: key to the chest, Aldric's heirloom dagger
- Scattered around: 12 gold pieces total, a tattered map of the local area (flavor item)

**DM Notes**:
- Grix is arrogant and taunts the players before combat. He speaks broken Common.
- If reduced to 5 HP or fewer, Grix attempts to flee through a crack in the back wall (DC 12 Dexterity/Athletics check to catch him). If he escapes, he does not have the key — he drops it.
- Victory condition: retrieve the chest and the heirloom dagger.

**Exits**: Back crack (Grix's escape route only) → wilderness (dead end for players)

---

### Scene 4: Return to Millhaven

**Setting**: Dawn. The players return to Millhaven with the recovered goods.

**Objective**: Deliver the chest and dagger to Aldric and Elder Mira. Collect the reward.

**DM Notes**:
- Aldric is overjoyed to see the dagger. He thanks the players effusively.
- Elder Mira pays the 50 gold reward without ceremony but with genuine respect.
- If players recovered both the chest AND the dagger: Aldric gives them an additional 10 gold from his personal purse.
- If any co-player was knocked unconscious during the adventure: Aldric arranges for the village healer to tend to them at no charge.
- End the session with a brief narration of the village celebrating and the players resting.

**Victory Condition**: Quest complete. Total possible reward: 50–60 gold + XP.

## Dungeon Master Guidance

- **Tone**: Keep it light and adventurous. This is a beginner adventure — don't make it feel overwhelming.
- **Pacing**: Each scene should take roughly 10–15 minutes of in-game time. Don't linger too long in Scene 1.
- **Difficulty**: If the party is struggling, have goblins flee or cower earlier than specified. If the party is breezing through, add an extra goblin or have Grix use Redirect Attack more aggressively.
- **Roleplay moments**: Encourage players to describe their actions vividly. Reward creative problem-solving (sneaking, distraction, negotiation) with advantage on rolls.
- **Beginner tips** (surface these when relevant):
  - Remind beginners they can take the Help action to give an ally advantage.
  - Remind beginners that healing potions can be used as a bonus action if they drink their own, or an action to administer to another.
  - Explain Armor Class and attack rolls the first time combat happens.
